## 🚦 UrbanFlow AI – Intelligent Adaptive Traffic Management System

UrbanFlow AI is an enterprise-level smart traffic management system powered by Artificial Intelligence (AI), Machine Learning (ML), Computer Vision, and Predictive Analytics. It is designed to reduce urban traffic congestion, improve road efficiency, and enable smarter city mobility.

Inspired by advanced traffic systems in countries like Japan and Singapore, the project is tailored to address real-world Indian traffic challenges such as fixed signal timings, congestion, emergency vehicle delays, and poor junction coordination.

The system dynamically optimizes traffic signals, predicts congestion patterns, detects violations and incidents, and recommends low-traffic routes in real time. It also supports emergency response through intelligent green corridor creation for ambulances.

UrbanFlow AI aims to build a fully adaptive traffic ecosystem that minimizes fuel wastage, enhances traffic flow, and improves overall urban transportation efficiency, making it a scalable solution for future smart cities.
